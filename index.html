UNIQUE-less-NESS

Future scenarios are possible, and even probable stories about the future. They are about understanding where we are. They challenge our perception and force us to question the predictable outcomes of the events taking place today.

UNIQUE-less-NESS is a possible future scenario which takes place in the year of 2050. It visualises future society, in which technological development is so advanced, that identity or more specifically human identity cease to exist.

With UNIQUE-less-NESS I question to what extend technological development will influence our perception of the surrounding world in the future, and what fundamental changes CAN and WILL bring to our daily lives.
